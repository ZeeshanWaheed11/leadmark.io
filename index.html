Everything You're Wondering

What tools do you use for cold email automation? ▼
We specialize in setting up and managing campaigns using Instantly and Smartlead - the industry's leading cold email platforms. We handle the complete technical setup, configuration, and optimization of these tools for your business.
Do I need to purchase the tools separately? ▼
Yes, you'll need your own subscriptions to Instantly or Smartlead (starting at $37-97/month), plus domain purchases and email hosting. We guide you through the most cost-effective options and handle all the technical setup. Total monthly tool costs typically range from $200-500 depending on scale.
How do you handle lead generation and data enrichment? ▼
We scrape and enrich data specifically for your ICP using premium tools and databases. This includes finding verified email addresses, LinkedIn profiles, company data, and other relevant information. We ensure all leads match your exact targeting criteria and are properly verified before campaigns.
What's included in your setup service? ▼
We provide end-to-end setup: domain configuration, DNS records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), email account creation, warming setup, campaign creation, email templates, tracking configuration, and initial optimization. Plus training on how to manage and scale your campaigns.
Can you manage our campaigns ongoing? ▼
Yes, we offer monthly management packages where we handle campaign optimization, A/B testing, list management, and performance monitoring. This includes weekly reports and strategy calls to continuously improve results.
How many leads can you generate for our ICP? ▼
Lead volume depends on your ICP specificity and market size. We typically deliver 1,000-10,000 verified leads per month. During onboarding, we'll analyze your market to provide accurate estimates for your specific niche.
What if my domain gets blacklisted? ▼
We use separate domains for cold outreach to protect your main domain. If any sending domain has issues, we handle blacklist removal and reputation recovery. Our proper setup and warming protocols minimize this risk significantly.
How long before we see results? ▼
Setup is complete in 48 hours, but proper warming takes 2-4 weeks. You'll see initial responses within days, but optimal results come after the warming period. Most clients achieve their target metrics within 30-45 days of launch.
