Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start sending?
Your infrastructure is ready in 48 hours. We recommend starting with our 14-day warming protocol for optimal results, but you can begin controlled sending immediately.
Do you work with Instantly and Smartlead?
Yes, perfectly. Your inboxes come pre-configured with correct SMTP/IMAP settings and authentication. They connect seamlessly to both platforms.

You do. While we manage and optimize everything, the domains and inboxes remain in your control. You can take them anywhere.

We monitor 24/7 and act immediately. We'll pause sending, rotate IPs, adjust warming, and run diagnostics until everything is green again.

Absolutely. Scale up anytime. Just purchase the domains and we'll have them deployed within 48 hours at the same $49/domain rate.

All packages include email support. Professional and Enterprise tiers get Slack access and weekly optimization calls with our deliverability experts.
