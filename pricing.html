Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your setup service? ▼
Our setup service includes complete configuration of your email infrastructure, platform setup (Instantly or Smartlead), DNS authentication services, warming protocol implementation, campaign template creation, and initial training. We handle all technical aspects so you can focus on your business. Domain purchases and tool subscriptions are separate costs we'll guide you through.
Do I need to buy tools separately from your services? ▼
Yes, you'll need your own subscriptions to Instantly or Smartlead ($37-97/month). Our service fees cover the expert setup, configuration, optimization, and management of these tools. We guide you through selecting the most cost-effective options and handle all the technical implementation.
How long does your setup service take? ▼
Our initial setup service is completed within 48 hours. This includes all technical configuration, platform setup, and initial testing. We recommend following our 2-4 week warming protocol service for optimal deliverability, during which we monitor and optimize your system daily.
Why do I need multiple domains for cold email? ▼
Multiple domains protect your main business domain's reputation while allowing you to scale. Each domain can safely send 100 emails/day without triggering spam filters. By spreading your volume across multiple domains, you maintain high deliverability rates and can reach more prospects. If one domain has issues, others continue working, ensuring consistent outreach.
What's the difference between email accounts and domains? ▼
A domain is your web address (like company.com), while email accounts are individual inboxes (like john@company.com). We set up 7 email accounts per domain for optimal rotation and deliverability. This structure allows each account to send 15-20 emails daily while maintaining a natural sending pattern that email providers trust.
How does email warming work? ▼
Email warming gradually builds your domain's reputation by slowly increasing sending volume over 2-4 weeks. We start with 5-10 emails per day, then systematically increase while monitoring engagement metrics. Our warming pools simulate real conversations between accounts, building trust with email providers. This process is essential for achieving 95%+ inbox placement rates.
What deliverability rates can I expect? ▼
With our infrastructure setup, clients typically achieve 95%+ inbox placement rates. This includes proper DNS authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), strategic IP rotation, professional warming protocols, and ongoing reputation monitoring. We continuously optimize your setup to maintain these high deliverability standards across all major email providers.
Can I upgrade my service package later? ▼
Absolutely! You can upgrade your service package anytime. We'll credit the difference from your current package toward the higher tier. Many clients start with our Foundation service to test our expertise, then upgrade to Accelerator once they see the quality of our work.
Do you provide the email domains or do I buy them? ▼
You purchase the domains (we recommend Namecheap or GoDaddy at ~$10/year per domain), and we handle everything else. We'll provide a list of recommended domain variations, guide you through the purchase process, and then configure all technical settings. This keeps domain ownership in your control while we manage the infrastructure.
What happens if a domain gets blacklisted? ▼
With our infrastructure setup, blacklisting is rare. However, if it occurs, we immediately isolate the affected domain, investigate the cause, and implement fixes. Your other domains continue operating normally, ensuring no disruption to your outreach. We also provide replacement domain setup at no extra charge within your support period.
Do you offer ongoing management services? ▼
Yes, we offer comprehensive monthly management services starting at $997/month. This includes campaign optimization, A/B testing services, list management, performance monitoring, and weekly strategy consultations. Our management service ensures your campaigns continuously improve and scale.
What's your refund policy for services? ▼
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all setup service packages. If you're not satisfied with our service quality within 30 days, we'll refund your service fee. This doesn't include third-party costs like domains or tool subscriptions, only our professional service fees.
